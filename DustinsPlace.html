Our Story

Dustin's Place was created in honor of Dustin Cullen who died in a single car accident on August 2nd, 2016. Dustin was first and foremost a God fearing man who loved his family and friends completely. Dustin worked with children his entire adult life at a residential facility and took great passion in the mental health of children and adolescents. After his death, Viki and their children Caythan, Liliana and Dean were left with a large void in their family. Through an amazing grief they were able to grieve his death and now Viki, along with some of Dustin's closeset friends and family, have come together to open Dustin's Place, Inc. The mission of Dustin's Place is to provide support in a safe place where grieving children, teens and their families can find connection and healing through sharing their experience of death of a love one. Our vision is that no one should have to walk through grief alone.
